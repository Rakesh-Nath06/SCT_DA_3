# SCT_DA_3

## HR Employee Attrition Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **HR Employee Attrition Dashboard** built using **Power BI** to analyze workforce turnover, identify key drivers of employee attrition, and provide strategic HR recommendations.

The dashboard is designed to answer the critical business question:

### **Why are employees leaving?**

By leveraging dynamic visualizations, KPIs, and filters, this dashboard enables HR professionals and business leaders to uncover actionable insights related to employee retention.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze employee attrition trends across departments, age groups, and salary categories
- Identify major factors influencing employee turnover
- Enable interactive filtering by:
  - Department
  - Age Group
  - Gender
  - Job Role
  - Marital Status
  - Education Field
  - Income Group
  - Business Travel
- Provide business recommendations to improve retention strategies

## Key Insights
- Employees aged **26–35** show the highest attrition
- **Overtime** strongly increases employee turnover
- **Low-income employees** are more likely to leave
- **Low job satisfaction** is highly correlated with attrition
- Early-career employees are more vulnerable to resignation

---

## Recommendations
- Reduce excessive overtime to improve work-life balance
- Introduce retention programs for younger employees
- Review salary structures for lower-income roles
- Improve employee engagement and job satisfaction
- Develop department-specific retention initiatives
